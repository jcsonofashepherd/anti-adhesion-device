"Anti - Adhesion Device" - Team 16
Economics
This device allows faster reoperations of open heart surgery, resulting in the reduction of costs for medical personnel and facilities for patients. The cardiac adhesions that are expected to form due to open heart surgery could take up to approximately one hour for surgeons to excise. The device takes less than one minute to prevent such complications. The device can be mass-produced using commercial syringes and plastic, the combination of which could be optimally manufactured so as to bring the device's cost low in the future.

Ultimately, the device lessens not only costs for the patient but also time spent and costs for the surgeons and hospitals.
