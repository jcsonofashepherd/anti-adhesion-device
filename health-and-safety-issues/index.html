Health and Safety Issues
This device makes use of an Aldehyde PEG solution, which is known to be toxic to humans. The toxic effects of this PEG is neutralized when the gel is fully reacted. However, when handling the unreacted component of the gel in the device, one must be cautious and use proper PPE.
