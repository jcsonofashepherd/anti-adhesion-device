"Anti - Adhesion Device" - Team 16
Overview of the Solution
The team produced a device that can apply the biomaterial in a consistent spray. The device keeps the two components of the biomaterial separate until they are ready for use, at which point they are mixed in a 1:1 ratio.

The device is comprised of interchangeable parts to facilitate replacement of clogged parts. The interchangeable parts include an adapter that guides the solutions from two syringes into a replaceable mixing tip that expels the mixed biomaterial.
