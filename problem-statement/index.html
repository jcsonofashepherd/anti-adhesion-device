"Anti - Adhesion Device" - Team 16
Problem Statement
An anti-adhesive biomaterial that prevents pericardial adhesions after open heart surgery clogs the standard duploject device used to administer the material due to its rapid gelation rate of two seconds, so an improved device suitable for the operating room is needed that minimizes the clogging issue and delivers the biomaterial in an evenly mixed ratio.
